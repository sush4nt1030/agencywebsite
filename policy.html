Privacy Policy

How Twilr Production collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic contact and project information including name, email, phone number, company/brand name, and project details provided via forms, email, or messages. We may also collect files you upload for project purposes (logos, briefs, assets).

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with third-party service providers (e.g., cloud storage, payment processors) strictly to perform project-related tasks. We choose providers that follow standard security practices.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data (secure file storage, password protection, limited access). However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure—clients should avoid sharing highly sensitive personal data unless necessary.

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies or basic analytics to improve user experience and measure traffic. You can disable cookies via your browser settings though some site features may be limited.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To do so, contact us at the address below. We will respond to reasonable requests in a timely manner.

7. Retention

We retain project and contact information for as long as needed to provide services and meet legal or business obligations. If you request deletion and no legal hold exists, we will remove your information from active systems.

8. Consent

By contacting or using our services, you consent to this Privacy Policy and to the collection and use of your information as described.
